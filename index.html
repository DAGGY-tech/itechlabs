About Itech Labs

Built in Uganda.
Built for the world.

Itech Labs is a premium creative and technology studio headquartered in Kampala, Uganda. Founded by Douglas, a builder and creative technologist with a mission to deliver world-class digital work from East Africa to global clients.

We believe where you're from should never limit what you can deliver. Every project we take on is treated like it matters — because to our clients, it does.
